What Is Etching?

Etching or "French embossing",is a popular technique developed during the mid-1800s that is still widely used in both residential and commercial spaces today.It is a subtle but elegant look that you will love. The chemicals in glass etching cream permanently "etch"the glass with micro-scratches to give it a frosted appearence.
